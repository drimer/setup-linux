============= MANUAL STEPS =============

Things that need to be manually customised by you:
1) Add, at least, one window to ~/.tmux.conf
2) Add pictures to wallpapoz
3) Log in with your user on Chromium to synchronize bookmarks and/or other things.
4) Make sure you have the correct permissions in the directories ~/Downloads/rtorrent_*
And if you wish:
5) Remove ~/install_wallpapoz
